Aboutus

Maval is a Town in Maval Taluka in Pune District of Maharashtra State, India. It belongs to Desh or Paschim Maharashtra region . It belongs to Pune Division . It is located 59 KM towards west from District head quarters Pune. It is a Taluka head quarter. Maval Pin code is 411045 and postal head office is N.I.A.

Maval town is located 59 km west of Pune. Nestled amidst the Sahyadri range, Maval abounds natural and historical marvels with hills, dams, old forts and ancient caves. The word Maval is derived from the Marathi word Maval, meaning the direction in which the sunsets. It is hilly terrain and part of the Sahyadri range/western ghats.
